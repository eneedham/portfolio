The Public Interest Law Center

Analyzing Substandard Housing

Over half of Philadelphia renters are rent-burdened and many suffer from substandard housing. Substandard housing was defined as properties with hazardous violations, were illegally rented, or had experienced evictions. We found that Limited Liability Corporations and shell companies are disproportionately responsible for issues of substandard housing while their properties are concentrated in communities of color. Our work called for pro-active violations inspections and open data regarding LLC ownership.
