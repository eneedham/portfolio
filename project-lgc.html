A Dream Deferred: Redlining and Structural Racism

Race and discriminatory policies, such as redlining, have shaped the landscape of Philadelphia and created devastating cycles of poverty that can last within families for generations. This exhibit brought the data and stories to life through an interactive touchscreen displaying historic patterns of industry, home ownership, and shifts in race/ethnicity, among other factors. The interactive application was paired with an art installation, documentary, and reflection booth.
