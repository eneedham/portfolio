Unequal Access to Playgrounds

Access to playgrounds is linked to childhood development and obesity rates. In Philadelphia, school playgrounds are more common in Center City and neighborhoods with a strong history of community-based activism. Communities of color and high poverty areas tend to have the fewest playgrounds.
