Fair Tech Collective

Air Quality in the Bay Area

This project was a component of the Fair Tech Collective’s “Meaning from Monitoring” initiative and investigated petrochemical pollution from five major refineries in the San Francisco Bay Area. We used data on refinery flaring activity and air toxin concentrations measured by fenceline monitors. Together with the Fair Tech Collective, we collaborated directly with community advocates in the Bay Area to hear their stories of impact from the refineries and their need for advocacy tools and information. For more information, checkout the Summer of Maps project page.
