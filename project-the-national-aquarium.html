The National Aquarium

Marine and Waterway Debris Modeling to Inform Cleanups

The National Aquarium works with the Ocean Conservancy's International Coastal Cleanup to organize volunteers for local debris clean up events. Volunteers record information about the amount and type of debris at each clean up location. Using this data, we modeled locations and factors related to marine and riverside debris accumulation. The findings will inform the locations of future clean ups.
