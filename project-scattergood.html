Place Matters: Risks & Assets to Children's Health & Well-being

Children’s health is impacted by exposure to risks and access to assets, such as quality parks and green space. We partnered with The Scattergood Foundation to showcase how these risks and assets are distributed in Philadelphia. I coordinated with a number of city agencies and nonprofits for data gathering and methodological input, ultimately creating health and well-being scores across the city. A primary goal was to inform decision makers and planners to spark a deeper city-wide conversation about how health is inherently linked to place. A rank was given to each Philadelphia City Council district and the final report was provided to each of Philadelphia’s seventeen City Council members, educating each about the primary risks, assets, and areas for opportunity to improve in their districts, and the city at-large.
